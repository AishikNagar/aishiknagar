Aishik Nagar - Tech Lead & Machine Learning Engineer

https://aishiknagar.com › about

I am a Tech Lead and Machine Learning Engineer at ASUS Global (AICS), leading the R&D of Clinical AI Assistant systems. My work focuses on Agentic LLMs, Generative AI, and building scalable healthcare solutions serving thousands of patients in dozens of hospitals. AICS is pretty much an AI startup within ASUS, and there is a lot of hardcore engineering and research which I'm involved in. This ranges from deep AI research (check out my papers and patents!) to taking the models we build into production with optimized GPU inference (both on cloud and on-prem in hospitals), agentic workflows and full stack apps, all integrated in Hospital Information Systems. Since its a small team, I lead everything from experiment design and implementation all the way to system design, software engineering and even some of the PM stuff from time to time (I honestly wouldn't like it any other way).

I also conduct independent research at the intersection of cognitive and embodied AI, multimodality, reasoning, mechanistic interpretability and clinical AI in my free time :D. I'm collaborating with a bunch of different organizations such as Center for Frontier AI Research, A*STAR and IN-CYPHER (Imperial/NTU CYber Protection for HEalthcaRe), with projects planned with other research agencies soon!

I graduated with a Bachelor of Engineering (EEE) from Nanyang Technological University as a Nanyang Science and Engineering Scholar. Most of my time is spent in programming, working on fun projects and solving challenging problems. I love building AI research, exploring new tech and building for social good. The wonders which technology can produce never ceases to amaze me.

I'm a huge sucker for sports like badminton, squash, tennis (any racket sport, really), swimming, cricket, soccer and running. In my free time, one can catch me watching comedy shows, drinking coffee and playing board games.
